ABOUT US


ANNTech is a Hong Kong based company specialized in system integration & supplying a wide range of industrial machinery, tools and electromechanical equipment's spare parts to a broad spectrum of industry applications which includes (but not limited to) Process Automation, MRO, Pneumatic, Motion Control, Electronic, Hydraulic, HVAC, Water Treatment and Electrical Control Equipment for manufacturing/process plant floor, Utility & facilities machinery. 

We are an Industrial Machinery & Supply Export Import Trading Company Supporting Your Business from Hong Kong since 2013...

Years of experience and familiarity with the problems relating to this sector enables us to supply a wide selection of certified products: new, refurbished, obsolete and repair. This is in order to satisfy the complex needs of the market and to help all our customers to keep their production operation running smoothly. 

Our mission at ANNTech is to be a value-added supplier to our customers. We partner with world-class companies that are dedicated to the relentless pursuit of product excellence. Our role is to listen, educate and respond to our client's needs. ANNTech is the cornerstone of communication between supplier and customer while providing product improvement and innovation in process design and systemization.​​

With an eye on emerging industries, ANNTech builts a strong reputation among customers and manufacturers for providing solutions, not just products, while continually focusing on friendly, courteous and efficient customer service.  

Teaming up with our customers and suppliers: Teamwork is the ability to work together toward a common vision. It is the ability to direct individual accomplishments toward organisational objectives. It is the fuel that allows common people to attain uncommon results.

​​ANNTech is a full service system integrator and supplier of world class industrial products and engineered systems to a wide range of industries and markets. ANNTech engages in services ranging from efficient and reliable distribution of products to fully integrated design, manufacturing and installation of complex process systems through extensive network of channel partners. Our core values focusing on partnerships with our customers and suppliers are as prevelent today as they were when ANNTech started its journey.​​​​

We hope you use the resources ANNTech provides to make informed decisions on all of your engineering and system requirements.
