ANNTech is a Hong Kong based company specialized in system integration & supplying a wide range of industrial machinery, tools and electromechanical equipment's spare parts to a broad spectrum of industry applications which includes (but not limited to) Process Automation, MRO, Pneumatic, Motion Control, Electronic, Hydraulic, HVAC, Water Treatment and Electrical Control Equipment for manufacturing/process plant floor, Utility & facilities machinery.

Our mission at ANNTech is to be a value-added supplier to our customers. We partner with world-class companies that are dedicated to the relentless pursuit of product excellence. Our role is to listen, educate and respond to our client's needs. ANNTech is the cornerstone of communication between supplier and customer while providing product improvement and innovation in process design and systemization.
